Remarks

The allowable values for metric type are:

'DOSE_VOLUME_CC_ROI' - Dose (Gy) covering Arg1 (cc) of the RoiName

'DOSE_VOLUME_MINUS_CC_ROI' - Dose (Gy) covering the total volume (cc) minus Arg1 (cc) of the RoiName

'DOSE_VOLUME_PERCENT_ROI' - Dose (Gy) covering Arg1 (%) of the RoiName

'VOLUME_CC_DOSE_ROI' - Volume (cc) of the RoiName covered by Arg1 (Gy)

'VOLUME_PERCENT_DOSE_ROI' - Volume (%) of the RoiName covered by Arg1 (Gy)

'VOLUME_CC_DOSE_RANGE_ROI' - Volume (cc) of the RoiName in range of Arg1 to Arg2 (Gy)

'VOLUME_PERCENT_DOSE_RANGE_ROI' - Volume (%) of the RoiName in range of Arg1 to Arg2 (Gy)

'MIN_DOSE_ROI' - Minimum dose (Gy) to the RoiName

'MAX_DOSE_ROI' - Maximum dose (Gy) to the RoiName

'MEAN_DOSE_ROI' - Mean dose (Gy) to the RoiName

'INTEGRAL_DOSE_ROI' - Integral dose (Gy * cc) to the RoiName

'MAX_DOSE' - Global maximum dose (Gy) over the enire dose grid

'VOLUME_OF_REGRET' - Total volume (cc) covered by Arg1 (Gy) but outside of the RoiName

'IRRADIATED_VOLUME' - Total volume (cc) covered by Arg1 (Gy)

'CONFORMATION_NUMBER' - Conformation number of the RoiName at Arg1 (Gy)

'CONFORMALITY_INDEX' - Conformality index of the RoiName at Arg1 (Gy)

'HOMOGENEITY_INDEX' - Homogeneity index of the RoiName at Arg1 (Gy)

'INHOMOGENEITY_INDEX' - Inhomogeneity index of the RoiName

'CUMULATIVE_METERSET' - Cumulative meterset

'ESTIMATED_BEAM_TIME' - Estimated beam time

'VOLUME'- Volume (cc) of the RoiName
